In Loving Memory of The Right Honourable Raila Amolo Odinga

7 January 1945 – 15 October 2025

On 15 October 2025, Odinga died of cardiac arrest in Koothattukulam, India at the age of 80.

Life & Legacy Highlights

Statesman & Prime Minister

Served as Kenya’s 2nd Prime Minister (2008–2013), Member of Parliament for Langata (1992–2013), and Leader of the Opposition (2013–2025).

Champion of Democracy

Endured detentions and advocated for multiparty democracy, constitutional reform, and devolution.

Continental Voice

AU High Representative for Infrastructure Development (2018–2023), advancing integration and connectivity across Africa.
