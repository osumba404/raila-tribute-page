Honours

National Honours

Elder of the Golden Heart (EGH), Kenya - National honour recognizing distinguished and exemplary service.

Honorary Degrees

University of Nairobi - Doctor of Laws (Honoris Causa), 2008.
Florida A&M University - Honorary Degree, 2012.
Limkokwing University - Doctorate of Leadership in Social Development, 2012.

Recognitions

Amnesty International Prisoner of Conscience (1991) - Recognized for enduring detention without trial in the struggle for multiparty democracy and human rights.
